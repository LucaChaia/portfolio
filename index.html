About Me

Welcome to my website!

I am a Computer Science student at CU Boulder who loves using math and computation to turn messy problems into clear, testable solutions. I build in Python/C++ and enjoy working where algorithms, data, and decision-making meet, from developing an AI-driven Mancala agent with minimax/alpha-beta and benchmarking performance or shipping custom software for local nonprofits through Blueprint Boulder. As President of the Data Buffs, I helped build a hands-on learning community focused on data science and led projects that translated real questions (including from CU Women's Basketball) into actionable analyses. My focus is on quantitative and analytical roles that model systems, iterate quickly, and solve novel problems with a rigorous, data-driven approach.
