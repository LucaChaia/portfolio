About Me

Welcome! I'm a Computer Science student at CU Boulder. I use math and computation to turn messy problems into clear solutions.

A lot of my favorite work has involved building solutions to real problems, from developing an AI-based Mancala project, creating software for local nonprofits through Blueprint Boulder, or helping grow a hands-on data science community through Data Buffs. I'm drawn to projects that are both technically challenging and require creative thinking.

Why I Made This Portfolio

This portfolio brings together the projects, experiences, and interests that have shaped my journey as a student. Here, you can easily explore what I've built and what drives me.

Building this site became a project in itself. I designed and built it from scratch using HTML/CSS and minimal JavaScript, then deployed it using Vercel. The portfolio is open source and can be found here.
